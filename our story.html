FAQ'S

Frequently Asked Questions

Can i find product measurements for each size?

add

Absolutely! Our Size Guide on each product page provides detailed measurements for each size. Measure yourself & use the guide to find your perfect fit.

How can i care for my garments to ensure longevity?

add

Generally, we recommend washing in cold water, avoiding bleach, and following specific care guidelines for delicate fabrics.

How long does it take to process a return or exchange?

add

Once we receive your return, it typically takes 20 business days to process. You'll receive an email confirmation once the process is complete.

Can i return an item if i've worn or used it?

add

Unfortunately, we can only accept returns for items in unused and unworn condition. Please ensure all tags are attached.

Can i return an item purchased during a sale or promotion?

add

Yes, items purchased during a sale or promotion can be returned. However, please note that certain exclusions may apply.

What payment methods do you accept?

add

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods. At checkout, you can select your preferred payment option.
